Ethereum-SEC-Bitcoin

A simple game of rock(Ethereum), paper(SEC), scissors(Bitcoin), with emphasis on technological promise in the shadow of regulation.

Ethereum (Rock) represents a promising public blockchain technology that is technologically more sophisticated than many other public blockchains, e.g. Bitcoin (Scissors).

The Securities and Exchange Commission (Paper) represents the chief regualatory body in the United States seeking to assert jurisidiction over new cryptocurrency issuances, including those offered on the ethereum blockchain (Rock).

Learn More

Bitcoin (scissors) represents the first public blockchain. It is technologically limited (non-Turing complete) versus Ethereum (Rock), but faces less regulatory uncertainty due in part to its incumbent status in the eyes of the SEC (Paper).
